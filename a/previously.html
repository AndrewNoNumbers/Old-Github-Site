routeFun

Built at a hackathon sponsored by MIT Media Lab, Volkswagen, and IDEO, I worked with Shawn Jain to create a tool that predicts the funness of roads by various data attributes collected through a car's on-board diagnostics. We presented to a panel of judges, including the head of global marketing of VW Group.

How it works

The tool pulls in four data fields from our OBD-II dataset and calculates it using the funness model we created. This generates an approximate funness rating for each segment of road which, when combined with smartphone GPS location, can be mapped with the Google Maps API.

My role

Prototyping, UI, web development
